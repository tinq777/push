🔑
Connect
your
GitHub.
Push needs a Personal Access Token to push files to your repos. It's stored only on your device — never sent anywhere else.
1
Go to GitHub.com → Settings Top right profile menu → Settings
2
Developer settings → Personal access tokens Choose "Tokens (classic)"
3
Generate new token — enable "repo" scope Copy the token — you'll paste it next
🔒 Your token is stored in your device's local storage only. Push never sends it anywhere except directly to GitHub's API.
