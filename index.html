🔑
Connect
your
platform.
Push needs a GitHub Personal Access Token to deploy your apps. It's stored only on your device — never sent anywhere except directly to GitHub.
1
Go to GitHub.com → Settings Top right profile menu → Settings
2
Generate a Personal Access Token Developer settings → Tokens (classic) → enable repo scope
3
Paste it into Push Tap the gear icon ⚙ on the home screen
🔒 Your token is stored locally on your device only. Push never stores or shares it anywhere.
