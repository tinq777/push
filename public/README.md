# ⚽ Mini's Kickoff — Match Tracker

A simple sideline app for grassroots kids football. Track the timer, score, substitutions and player time — then share a match report with parents in one tap.

---

## Installing on Your Phone

Mini's Kickoff works like a native app when added to your home screen. Here's how:

**iPhone (Safari only):**
1. Open the app URL in **Safari** — it won't work if added from Chrome
2. Tap the **Share** button at the bottom of the screen (box with an arrow pointing up)
3. Scroll down and tap **Add to Home Screen**
4. Name it **Mini's Kickoff** and tap **Add**

The app will appear on your home screen with a green icon. Tap it to open full screen with no browser bars, just like a regular app.

**Android (Chrome):**
1. Open the app URL in **Chrome**
2. Tap the **three dot menu** in the top right
3. Tap **Add to Home Screen**
4. Tap **Add**

## Getting Started

Open the app and you'll see three tabs at the top — **Match**, **Squad** and **POTM**. You can tap the tabs or **swipe left and right** to navigate between them.

Before kickoff, set up your squad in the **Squad tab** and choose your half length and sub alert settings on the **Match tab**.

---

## Match Tab

This is your main screen during the game.

- Tap the team names to rename them before kickoff
- Hit **▶ Start** to begin the countdown timer
- Tap **+** next to your team's score to log a goal and select who scored
- Tap **+** next to the opponent's score to add their goal instantly
- Tap **−** to remove a goal if entered by mistake
- Use **⏸ Pause** during half time
- Hit **▶ 2nd Half** to start the second half
- If the game ends early tap **🏁 End Early**

**Before kickoff** you can set:
- **Half length** — 5, 10, 15, 20, 25, 30 or 45 minutes
- **Sub alert threshold** — how big the time gap needs to be before a substitution is suggested (1–5 mins)
- **Sub alerts on/off** — toggle the substitution suggestion feature on or off

Once the match has started, scores and team names are locked and cannot be changed until the match ends.

---

## Squad Tab

Manage your players here. You can also swipe to this tab from the Match tab.

- **On Field** — players currently playing. Tap **Sub Off** to bring them off
- **On Bench** — players waiting. Tap **Sub On** to bring them on
- Each player shows their field time and bench time
- Tap **✎** to edit a player's name (only available before kickoff)
- Tap **🤒** to mark a player as absent before kickoff — they'll be ignored for substitutions and won't appear during the match
- Tap **Restore** to bring an absent player back
- Add new players using the box at the bottom (only available before kickoff)
- Duplicate player names are not allowed

When a substitution is suggested, the player who should come off will **flash orange** and the player who should come on will **flash blue** for 5 seconds.

Squad editing is locked once the match has started.

---

## Substitution Suggestions

The app automatically suggests substitutions to keep playing time fair for every player. When the gap between a field player and a bench player reaches your set threshold, an alert flashes on the Match tab for 5 seconds then stays visible as a reminder.

- Tap **Sub Off** on the alert to apply the substitution instantly
- Or ignore it and make your own decision — you're always in control
- Absent players are never included in substitution suggestions
- You can turn sub suggestions off entirely using the toggle in the settings before kickoff

---

## POTM Tab

Available only after the match has ended. Select your **Player of the Match** from a grid of all players.

- Tap a player card to select them
- Optionally add a photo by tapping **📷 Add Photo**
- Tap **View Match Summary** to see the full report

---

## Match Summary

The summary shows the final score, result, goals scored, Player of the Match photo, each player's time on the field and any absent players.

**Sharing with parents:**
- Tap **Share as Image** — generates a clean formatted image you can send directly via WhatsApp, iMessage or any app. The image includes the score, POTM photo, goals and player times
- Tap **Share as Text** — sends a plain text version of the match report

---

## Tips

- Set your squad up before the season — player names are saved automatically to your device
- Mark players as 🤒 absent before kickoff so subs are calculated correctly
- Swipe left and right to switch between tabs quickly during the match
- The app works best in **Safari on iPhone** — add it to your home screen for the full app experience
- Each device saves its own data locally — squad info won't carry over to another phone
- Once the match starts, the squad and scores are locked to prevent accidental changes
